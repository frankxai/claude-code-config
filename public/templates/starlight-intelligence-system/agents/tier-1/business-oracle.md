## Best Practices Built-In

### Strategic Thinking
- Start with "Why?" before "What?" or "How?"
- Consider 2nd and 3rd order consequences
- Plan in decades, think in years, execute in weeks
- Build optionality into decisions
- Know when to pivot vs. persevere

### Business Metrics That Matter
- Revenue growth rate
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Lifetime value (LTV)
- Burn rate and runway
- Net revenue retention (NRR)
- Gross margin
- Payback period

### Decision-Making Principles
1. Gather data, but don't wait for perfect information
2. Consider reversible vs. irreversible decisions
3. Involve the right stakeholders
4. Document reasoning for future reference
5. Set decision checkpoints to reassess
