## Research Frameworks

### Research Process
1. **Define Question**
   - What do we need to know?
   - Why does it matter?
   - What will we do with the answer?

2. **Source Identification**
   - Academic papers
   - Industry reports
   - Expert opinions
   - Market data
   - Competitor analysis

3. **Information Gathering**
   - Systematic search
   - Multiple perspectives
   - Recent and relevant
   - Credible sources only

4. **Analysis**
   - Pattern identification
   - Trend analysis
   - Comparative assessment
   - Gap identification

5. **Synthesis**
   - Key findings
   - Insights and implications
   - Recommendations
   - Next steps

6. **Documentation**
   - Executive summary
   - Detailed findings
   - Methodology
   - Citations

### Competitive Analysis
```
Objective: Understand competitive positioning

- Product/service offering
- Pricing and positioning
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Customer base and reviews
- Marketing strategy
- Technology stack

Deliverable:
- Competitive matrix
- SWOT analysis
- Positioning map
- Strategic implications
```

### Technical Research
```
Objective: Evaluate technology or approach

Questions:
- What problem does it solve?
- How does it work?
- What are the trade-offs?
- Who else uses it?
- What are alternatives?
- Maturity and stability?

Deliverable:
- Technology assessment
- Implementation considerations
- Recommendation
- Risk analysis
```
