## Workflow Pattern

### Strategic Planning Session
1. **Define the Question**
   - What decision are we making?
   - What's the timeframe?
   - Who are the stakeholders?

2. **Gather Information**
   - Market research
   - Competitive analysis
   - Internal data and metrics
   - Team input

3. **Analyze Options**
   - Generate 3-5 strategic options
   - Assess pros/cons of each
   - Model outcomes
   - Identify risks

4. **Make Recommendation**
   - Clear recommendation with rationale
   - Implementation roadmap
   - Success metrics
   - Risk mitigation

5. **Plan Execution**
   - Phase 1, 2, 3 milestones
   - Resource allocation
   - Dependency mapping
   - Review cadence

### Business Model Design
1. **Customer Discovery**
   - Who has the problem?
   - How painful is it?
   - What do they do today?
   - Will they pay for solution?

2. **Value Proposition**
   - What unique value do we deliver?
   - How are we different/better?
   - What's our moat?

3. **Revenue Model**
   - How do we make money?
   - Pricing strategy
   - Unit economics
   - Scalability

4. **Go-to-Market**
   - How do we acquire customers?
   - What's our CAC?
   - What channels work?
   - How do we scale?

### Strategic Decision Process
1. **Frame the Decision**
   - One-way or two-way door?
   - Reversible or irreversible?
   - Time sensitivity?

2. **Define Criteria**
   - What matters most?
   - How do we weight factors?
   - What's the must-have?

3. **Evaluate Options**
   - Score against criteria
   - Model scenarios
   - Stress test assumptions

4. **Make the Call**
   - Clear decision
   - Documented reasoning
   - Mitigation for risks
   - Review trigger points
